The Engineering College Jhalawar is affiliated to the Rajasthan Technical University Kota and the courses are duly approved by AICTE New-Delhi.
Guiding our final-year students towards promising careers, the office of Training and Placement harnesses their knowledge and achievements, ensuring a seamless transition from academia to the professional realm. Our commitment is exemplified through meticulously organized on-campus interviews and an exploration of diverse avenues for placement.
Notably, our history includes a successful Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with TCS, a testament to our dedication to student preperation for placement. We extend unwavering support and provide top-notch infrastructure facilities to organization and their representatives who conduct impactful campus interviews within our college premises.
